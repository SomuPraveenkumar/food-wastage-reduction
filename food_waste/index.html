Our Mission to Reduce Food Waste

At FoodSave, we're revolutionizing food distribution by connecting surplus meals from restaurants and food businesses with communities in need through our innovative platform.

Every day, millions of meals go to waste while thousands face food insecurity. Our intelligent matching system bridges this gap by connecting verified food donors with local NGOs and food banks in real-time. We handle the entire process from pickup coordination to safe delivery, ensuring surplus food reaches those who need it most.

Quick Response

Average 2-hour pickup time from notification to collection

Quality Assurance

All donations undergo strict safety checks

Since our founding in 2023, we've redirected over 1.2 million meals from landfills to dinner tables, partnering with 500+ food establishments and 200+ charitable organizations nationwide. Our technology-driven approach includes AI-powered surplus prediction and optimized routing systems to maximize food rescue efficiency.
